About Me

I am an Astronomy graduate from Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia. In astrophysics, I focus on the study of the Star-Forming Main Sequence (SFMS), analyze its evolution across cosmic time to study the star formation process and galaxy evolution. I examine the galactic physical properties by analyzing the spectral energy distribution (SED).

As a Master of Science in Astronomy, I am used to performing data processing, analysis, and visualization using Python. I also have basic skills in machine learning. Studying astrophysics led me to have strong analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, a data-driven decision-making mindset, and a high sense of curiosity that help me to learn new things.
